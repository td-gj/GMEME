# Game Features

GMEME Arena is a futuristic Web3 battle game where strategy meets meme culture. Here is comprehensive guide to all game features.

## 1. The Arena (Active Battles)
This is the heart of the game. Here you can join live battles or watch others fight.

- **Join Queue**: Select your fighter to enter the matchmaking queue.
- **Entry Fee**: **10 GMEME** per battle.
- **Matchmaking**: The system automatically pairs you with the next available opponent.
- **Rewards**:
  - **Winner**: Gets **18 GMEME** (90% of the pot).
  - **Platform Fee**: 10% goes to the development & voting pool.
- **Free Daily Vote**: Everyone gets **1 FREE vote per day** in the Live Arena!

## 2. Mint Fighter (Create NFT)
Before you can fight, you need a champion.

- **Cost**: **1 GMEME** per mint.
- **Upload**: Choose any image to represent your fighter. We upload it securely to IPFS.
- **Metadata**: Your fighter is minted as a unique ERC-721 NFT on the Polygon blockchain.
- **Starting Stats**: All fighters start with **Rank: Bronze** and **ELO: 0**.

## 3. GME Battles (No Entry Fee)
Experience battle mode without spending any GMEME! Perfect for new players to practice and earn rewards.

### How It Works
- **Entry Fee**: **FREE** (No GMEME required to join)
- **Requirement**: You must hold at least **1 GMEME** in your wallet to participate (anti-spam protection)
- **Daily Limit**: **3 battles per day** per wallet
- **Battle Duration**: **5 Minutes** (Quick battles!)
- **Matchmaking**: Same as premium battles - auto-paired with available opponents

### Rewards
- **Winner**: Earns **10 GME** tokens
- **Winning Voters**: Each voter who backed the winner receives **0.1 GME**
- **Losing Voters**: No rewards (0 GME)

### What is GME?
**$GME** is our utility token for the GME Battles mode.

- **Use Case**: Used for rewards in GME Free Battles
- **Airdrops**: $GME will be used for future community airdrops
- **Migration**: $GME tokens will be **migrated to become the main $GMEME token** in a future update. Hold your $GME to receive the official $GMEME airdrop!

## 5. Leaderboard & Ranking System
See who is the strongest fighter in the metaverse. Rankings are based on **ELO** points.

- **How to earn ELO**:
  - **Win**: +1 ELO
  - **Lose**: -1 ELO (Minimum 0)

- **Rank Tiers**:
  - **Bronze**: 0 - 5 ELO
  - **Silver**: 6 - 10 ELO
  - **Gold**: 11 - 20 ELO
  - **Platinum**: 21 - 50 ELO
  - **Diamond**: 51 - 100 ELO
  - **Legendary**: 100+ ELO

- **Updates**: Rankings update automatically after every battle result on Polygon Mainnet.

## 6. My Collection
Manage your roster of fighters.

- View all NFTs you own.
- Check their current Rank and ELO.
- Select which fighter to send into the Arena.

## 7. Voting (Spectator Mode)
Even if you don't fight, you can earn.

- **Vote**: Bet on who you think will win an active battle.
- **Requirement**: You must hold at least **1 GMEME** in your wallet to cast a vote (Anti-spam protection).
- **Free Daily Vote**: Get **1 FREE vote every day** - use it wisely!
- **Reward**: If your choice wins, you share a pool of GMEME tokens distributed to correct voters.
