## About GME Token

**$GME** is the utility token for the GME Free Battle mode.

### What is GME for?
- **Free Battle Rewards**: Winners of GME Free Battles earn **10 GME** per victory.
- **Voter Rewards**: Voters who back the winning fighter receive **0.1 GME**.
- **Airdrop Future**: $GME will be used for future airdrops and community rewards.

### Migration to GMEME
> **Important**: $GME tokens will be **migrated to become the main $GMEME token** in a future update. Hold your $GME tokens to receive the official $GMEME airdrop when the migration occurs.

## Contract Parameters

### GMEME Battle
- **Vote Cost**: `0.06 GMEME` (Paid per vote) - **FREE 1 vote/day**
- **Minimum Hold**: `1 GMEME` (Required to cast a vote)
- **Battle Duration**: `24 Hours`
- **Entry Fee**: `10 GMEME`
- **Rewards**:
  - Winner: `18 GMEME`
  - Voters (Win/Lose): `1 GMEME` / `0.2 GMEME`
  - Dev Fee: `0.8 GMEME`

### GME Battle 
- **Entry Fee**: `FREE` (No cost to join)
- **Requirement**: Hold at least **1 GMEME** in wallet to join
- **Daily Limit**: **3 battles per day** per wallet
- **Battle Duration**: `5 Minutes`
- **Rewards**:
  - Winner: **10 GME**
  - Winning Voters: **0.1 GME** each
  - Losing Voters: **0 GME**
